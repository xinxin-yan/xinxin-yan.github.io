About Me

Hi! I'm Xinxin, a linguist specializing in pragmatics and the comprehension of comparison statements. My research explores the cognitive and experimental aspects of how people interpret similes and metaphors. I am particularly interested in bridging theoretical linguistics with empirical methodologies.
