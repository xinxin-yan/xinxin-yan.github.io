About Me

Hi! I'm Xinxin, a linguist specializing in theoretical and experimental pragmatics. My research have been focusing on studying how people interpret and produce figurative language, such as similes, metaphors and metonymies, using diverse experimental methods (e.g., reaction-time studies, eye-tracking). I am currently leading an NLP project aimed at building computational models capable of automatically detecting multiword (extended) metaphor and simile expressions.
